Hi, I'm Leontine, welcome to my portfolio. It's definitely a work in progress. Follow me on my journey as I learn and develop. I recently graduated from the web development program at TechGrounds and wish to continue my development as a frontender. My goal is to perfect my knowledge of HTML, CSS and Vanilla Javascript. My ambition includes becoming an animation master! I like to make my work look visually appealing and have discovered my creative side. I want to make things that look amazing! As a person, I am soft spoken and soft hearted. I love a bit of humor, cute cats, delicious coffee and original flavors jelly beans.
